SOFA beds are the most convenient piece of furniture around. Two pieces of furniture for the price of one - what’s not to love?

Whether you need to save space in your tiny city studio or simply want a bed for friends when they stay the night, one of the best cheap sofa beds can be such a saviour.

Don’t get put off by the thought of cheap sofa beds, though - gone are the days of complicated mechanisms, and uncomfortable sleep on dodgy mattresses.

Back in the day, you could immediately tell a sofa bed from an ordinary sofa, because they were boxy and uncomfortable. But today, there’s little between them. A sofa bed can enhance your interior aesthetic like any other piece of furniture.

Where can I buy a cheap sofa bed?

There is a range of retail outlets online that you can buy a cheap sofa bed from, to suit your style, needs and budget.

High-street wise, brands like Argos, Ikea and M&S offer a range of sofa beds - as does online furniture retailers Made and Dunelm.

How to make a sofa bed more comfortable

Sofa beds, though they may be super versatile, space-saving and easy-to-use - you can run the risk of being a little uncomfortable if you're used to a thick, memory foam mattress on your usual bed.

Your best bed - aside from plumping your sofa bed up with super-soft blankets and cushions, is to purchase a memory foam mattress topper.

We've found some affordable ones on Amazon that are bound to suit your budget and needs.

How to a wash sofa bed

We get it, accidents happen! Your best bet is to check the supplier's instructions on the best way to wash your sofa bed, first.

Some sofa beds may even have removable, machine-washable covers, too, to make your life even easier - often faux leather sofa beds are wipe clean, too.

Otherwise, we recommend vacuuming your sofa bed frequently - and using soap and warm water, or baby wipes, to gently pat down and scrub away any stains or dirt, then leaving it to airdry.

Can you sleep on a sofa bed every night?

There's no reason why you can't sleep on a sofa bed every night, as long as your comfortable and it's providing you with adequate support.

However, most people find that they sleep better in a bed. Check out our round-up of the best beds here.

What is a click clack sofa bed?

A click clack sofa bed is generally a simple type of sofa bed that gets its name from the sound it makes when it's folded and unfolded.

Click clack beds tend to have fewer cushions and smaller arm rests to make it easier to transform them from bed to sofa and visa versa.

England fans convinced breakthrough star should START for Three Lions as 'missing piece' in Southgat

June 06, 2024
blog

ENGLAND fans are convinced that Kobbie Mainoo is the “missing piece” in the England jigsaw after his superb performance this afternoon.

Erik ten Hag handed the 18-year-old his first Premier League start for Manchester United at Goodison Park.

And the midfielder produced a brilliant performance in midfield as the Red Devils romped to a 3-0 win over the Toffees.

Alejandro Garnacho stole the headlines with his incredible overhead kick, but it was his young team-mate that had supporters of the Three Lions talking.

Mainoo, who has represented England from Under-17 level to U19, completed 34 of his 41 passes to keep United ticking over.

He also won five duels, recovered the ball three times and made two clearances - including a vital one off the line while it was still 1-0.

Mainoo made his debut in the Carabao Cup win over Charlton back in January and then came off the bench in the Premier League victory over Leicester City.

He was then part of United’s squad for the pre-season tour, but an injury in the summer ruled him out of action until today’s return.

And despite it only being his first start, some supporters are convinced that he could be the man to line-up alongside Declan Rice and Jude Bellingham as they urged Gareth Southgate to pick him for England ahead of Euro 2024.

One viewer responded to his display by posting on social media: “England’s missing piece between Declan Rice and Jude Bellingham is Kobbie Mainoo.  Gareth, it’s your turn now.”

Who is Romeo Beckham's girlfriend Mia Regan?

August 30, 2024
blog

ROMEO Beckham's life has always played out in the spotlight, and his love life is no different.

The young Becks has not shortage of admirers, but it is Mia Regan who has won his heart.

Who is Mia Regan?

Mia Regan is a London-based model originally from Chip, near Bath, born on November 19, 2002

She is managed by Storm modelling and was scouted at Birmingham Clothes Show.

The young beauty is described on Storm’s website as “the quintessential millennial muse with a unique gen-z approach to fashion and beauty”.

Mia is probably best known by her @mimimoocher Instagram account, where she keeps fans up-to-date on her popular handle.

She has worked with brands like Balmain, Gucci Beauty, and British Vogue.

Mia has also worked for boyfriend Romeo Beckham's mum Victoria Beckham’s fashion line, and launched a denim capsule collection in 2022.

The young star regularly attends fashion’s biggest shows and has appeared at both Paris Fashion Week and New York Fashion Week.

Meanwhile, her boyfriend Romeo was also previously a model, and worked for brands like Burberry.

But he has now followed in his famous father’s footsteps, and is a footballer - playing for Brentford Under 21s.

In a rare interview in 2022, Romeo told puma-catchup.com: “I’ve grown up around football, so it was always a big interest for me and when I started playing more seriously it became a goal.

“I played other sports too but decided football was the route I wanted to go down.”

When did Romeo Beckham and Mia Regan start dating?

Romeo and Mia became an official couple on May 3, 2019.

It is unclear how the two met, but most likely it would have been through running in the same social media circles.

Ever since the pair made their relationship public around September 2019, the love birds have been posting cute pic after cute pic.

They were separated during the coronavirus lockdown as they were quarantined with their respective parents, but on July 27, 2020, they shared a snap-together of them both holidaying in Italy with Beckham's family.

Why did Romeo Beckham and Mia Regan break up?

In July 2022, the couple called it quits due to their busy schedules.

Romeo was pursuing his career as a professional football player in the US, while Mia was busy modelling in the UK.

A source told the Daily Mail that maintaining a “long-distance relationship took its toll” on the pair.

They said: “They’re both lovely young people and remain very fond of each other, and the family love Mia."

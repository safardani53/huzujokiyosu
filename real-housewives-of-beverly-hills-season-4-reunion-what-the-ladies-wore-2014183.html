Real Housewives of Beverly Hills Season 4 Reunion: What The Stars Wore

February 09, 2024
blog

The ladies of Real Housewives of Beverly Hills were ready to bring it to the three-part season four reunion show, which aired its first installment on March 17. In between shade-throwing, eye rolling, and tears flowing, we were mesmerized by the ladies’ head-to-toe looks.

Here, check out a style break down of the housewives’ reunion show dresses, with the help of the eagle eyes at BigBlondeHair.com.

On one side of the reunion show sat the more colorful stars. Yolanda Foster showed off her birthday bob in an orange Victoria Beckham dress ($2195) with black short sleeves. Perhaps the Dutch model’s style sensibility rubbed off on newcomer Carlton Gebbia, who surprised us with a demure Emilio Pucci dress ($2160) with lace sleeves.

Fellow Brit Lisa Vanderpump wore her trademark pink in a look-at-me fuchsia shade with a Monique Lhuillier lace dress ($2395). She paired the feminine style with glam waves, lots of lashes, and a natural lip color.

In between dodging digs from Brandi Glanville about her “ice skater” dress (“Sochi’s calling!”), the season’s other newbie, Joyce Giraud, sparkled in a strapless Sherri Hill dress with a gold pleated skirt ($398). While we’re a sucker for anything with a sequin, the strapless design and twirly skirt do remind us of figure skating.

On the opposite side sat Kyle Richards, who showed off her curves in an eggplant Herve Leger dress ($1150). She rocked a Kate Middleton-esque blowout and a smoky eye and pink lipstick. Sister Kim Richards joined her; she opted for the show’s most budget-friendly look with the Dress the Population Lola dress in navy with gold swirls ($195).

ALTERNATIVE country singer Brandi Carlile is a self-taught musician with a knack for crossing genres.

Over the span of her career, Brandi has earned 18 Grammy nominations with six official wins.

Who is Brandi Carlile?

Born on June 1, 1981, Brandi Carlile is a native of Washington state.

At just five years old, the acclaimed musician contracted bacterial meningitis - nearly killing her.

She began singing as a child and teaching herself guitar and piano as a teenager.

Brandi dropped out of Tahoma High School to pursue a career in music.

Throughout the early 2000s, Brandi performed around Seattle and was signed to Columbia Records in 2004.

Her self-titled debut album came out the following year.

Is Brandi Carlile married?

In June 2012, Brandi Carlile announced her engagement to longtime partner Catherine Shepherd.

The two married in Massachusetts on September 15 of the same year.

Since 2005, Brandi has released seven solo studio albums:

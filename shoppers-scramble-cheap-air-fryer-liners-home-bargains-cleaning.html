IF you have joined the air fryer crew, you’re in luck, as we’ve got just the thing for you.

Whilst air fryers are a brilliant kitchen appliance that make cooking much quicker and easier, cleaning the much-loved tool can be a bit of a challenge.

But thankfully, Home Bargains have come to the rescue, leaving many shoppers open-mouthed.

One bargain hunter, Audrey Marie Rose, was left very impressed when she spotted a brilliant bargain buy whilst looking on Facebook.

Taking to a private group, Extreme Couponing and Bargains UK group, which has 2.2million members, Audrey shared the cheap find.

Home Bargains are currently selling reusable air fryer liners, which will save food fans lots of time and effort cleaning their kitchen gadget.

The air fryer liners come in a pack of two and will fit most circular and square fryers.

The liners will cost you just £1.29 and are non-stick and easy to clean.

The holes allow air to circulate whilst cooking, meaning you still get that desired all round cooked result.

The liner will protect the basket from cooked food, so there’ll be no more spending ages standing at the sink scrubbing away at your air fryer thanks to these bargain buys.

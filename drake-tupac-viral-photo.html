Did Drake know Tupac and what is the viral photo?

September 14, 2024
blog

A PICTURE of Drake allegedly standing behind with Tupac has been circulating online - but is it real, and did the Canadian rapper ever meet the late artist?

Fans have been scratching their heads after the photo went viral on social media.

Did Drake know Tupac?

Drake has never met Tupac but has made shout outs to the legendary rapper, including one in the hit song One Dance.

What is the viral photo?

The photo, which appears on Twitter, is said to show Tupac with Drake during a chance encounter in 1986.

The picture, which was uploaded to the social media network on February 15, 2022, appears to show Tupac holding a fake handgun with a man in the background who looked like Drake.

But the man, whose real identity is unknown, is seen walking past the frame when the picture was taken.

It is unclear who took the picture, or when it was taken, despite the 1986 date some pundits have suggested.

Eagle-eyes fans were quick to suggest the man in the background was Drake but, if the 1986 date is to believed, then that would have made the Canadian rapper a baby, as he was born in the same year.

And if it snap had been taken no later than 1996, that would have meant Drake would have just been 10 years old.

Tupac died in 1996 in Las Vegas, Nevada, when he was gunned down by an unknown assailant.

According to CNN, Las Vegas retired sergeant Chris Carroll was there for the rapper's last words.

He told the outlet: "He looked at me, and he took a breath to get the words out, and he opened his mouth, and then the words came out: ‘F**k you.’”

Much of Tupac's music is still widely listened to and celebrated.

Drake is a Canadian rapper, singer and actor whose hits include Nice For What (2018), Child's Play (2016) and Hold On, We're Going Home featuring Majid Jordan (2013). Others include Portland (2017), Know Yourself (2015) and Laugh Now Cry Later featuring Lil Durk (2020).

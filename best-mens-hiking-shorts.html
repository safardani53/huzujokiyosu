10 Best Men's Hiking Shorts 2022 | The Sun UK

April 17, 2024

GOT a summer adventure on the cards? The best men's hiking shorts are just the ticket for your warm-weather escapades and the right pair will quickly become a hiking essential.

They should be super comfortable to wear, give you plenty of breathing room and many are even designed with useful compartments and the ability to withstand a summer downpour.

What kind of shorts are best for hiking?

There are many fabrics, styles and features of hiking shorts available for men right now - so it can feel a little overwhelming when you're faced with the choice.

Whether you're on the hunt for something lightweight and breathable, you need water and UV protection, or you're after a pair of hiking shorts that will withstand serious wear and tear (and save any nasty chafing) – there's something for you in our selection.

And for hikers who can't decide whether they want trousers or shorts for their next outdoor adventure, you can even snap up a pair of zippable trouser/shorts, so you can get the best of both worlds.

What are the best hiking shorts for men?

This very much depends on your needs and your budget – but a general rule of thumb is they should fit well and feel comfy to wear, they should have a fair amount of stretch in them so it's easy to walk with no discomfort and they should be durable and ideally made from a sweat-wicking, water-repellant fabric.

Here's our selection of some of the best hiking shorts on the market right now – whatever your style and budget, there's something for you here.

We've tested out these hiking shorts from Columbia over a couple of months and found them to be excellent for easy summer hiking.

But if you're planning on walking nice trails in the heat, these are perfect as they're lightweight and moisture wicking which will keep you cool.

  • Decathlon Quechua Men's Hiking Long Shorts - MH500, £24.99 from Decathlon - buy here

Designed by a team of passionate mountain trekkers, these affordable men's hiking shorts are thoughtfully constructed, with details that only come with experience.

Comfort is key here as the shorts are super stretchy, with 15% elastane and a slit at the bottom to provide maximum freedom of movement. They're also lightweight, breathable and wick away perspiration swiftly.

The shorts feature an elasticated waistband, a removable belt and there are three zipped pockets, including one at the thigh.

Made by Quechua, a brand that has a design centre at the foot of Mont-Blanc, these well-priced shorts have been made with serious hiking in mind.

Rab Men's Oblique Short

  • Rab Men's Oblique Short, £50 from Rab - buy here 

While strictly speaking these Rab shorts are designed for climbing, their next-level flexibility and lightweight feel make them perfect for almost all outdoor adventures.

Long, slim and with a fuss-free style, they're one of the best-looking pair of shorts in our edit and they come in four colours, including Nightfall Blue (pictured) and a punchy Cumin.

Columbia Men's Silver Ridge II Cargo Shorts

These sleek shorts from outdoor specialists Columbia have been hailed as the perfect find for hikers in need of lightweight shorts that can be worn year-round.

They're made from Omi-wick technology, which is breathable and fast drying, and there's an emphasis on getting a great fit as they have a built-in belt and adjustable inner belt to get them feeling exactly right.

Made from 100% nylon, they're also perfect for rolling up tight and packing away into your backpack or luggage, making them a great travel companion.

For serious hikers, Arc'teryx are a go-to brand. And these top-rated Gamma shorts deliver on all of the brand's lofty promises according to its happy reviewers.

Made from quick-drying TerraTex stretch nylon fabric, they're wind resistant, durable and they dry fast.

That ’90s Show has finally arrived on Netflix, introducing fans to a new ensemble of young and talented actors. One of the actors is Mace Coronel, who you may know from his days on Nickelodeon.

Like his co-star Callie Haverda, Mace’s character has a connection to the That ’70s Show characters. Get to know this 18-year-old breakout star now that the show is available to stream on Netflix.

1. Mace plays Jay Kelso in That ’90s Show.

Mace is the son of Michael Kelso and Jackie Burkhart, played by Ashton Kutcher and Mila Kunis. Like his dad, Jay is very smooth with the ladies. He isn’t the only That ’70s Show kid. Callie plays Leia Forman, the daughter of Eric and Donna.

2. Mace rose to fame on Nickelodeon as a kid.

Mace played Ricky Harper in the hit Nickelodeon series Nicky, Ricky, Dicky and Dawn from 2014 to 2018. He also appeared in the TV series Zoe Valentine. His latest role before That ’90s Show was in the Netflix series Colin in Black & White.

3. Mace released a short film in 2022.

Mace released his short film GIBSON on his YouTube channel in April 2022. “Filmed within the month of June 2021. GIBSON is an experimental short film that provides a glimpse of life on a hot summer day outside of ‘the strip’ in Las Vegas,” he wrote. He filmed the short when he was 16 years old. He links to his short in his Instagram bio.

4. Mace can speak Dutch.

Mace is fluent in English and Dutch. He revealed on Instagram in 2021 that he got his Dutch passport and wrote the caption in Dutch.

5. Mace is on Instagram.

Mace is on Instagram under the handle @macecoronel. He has over 1 million followers.

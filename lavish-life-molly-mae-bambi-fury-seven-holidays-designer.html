SHE’S not even one years old but Molly-Mae Hague’s baby Bambi Fury is already an international jet-setter. 

From super yachts, to first class flights, the youngster - who is the first child of Love Island couple Molly-Mae and boxer Tommy Fury - has already been on seven luxury holidays with her glamorous parents.

Not only this, but she’s got a fabulous bedroom that many would be envious of, as well as a stunning designer wardrobe full of fabulous clothes. 

Here, we take a closer look at the lavish life of Bambi Fury - so, prepare to be jealous. 

A-list trips

Maldives - December

Molly and Tommy are currently enjoying a £10,000-a-night break to the Maldives, and are staying in a stunning overwater bungalow at the Ritz Carlton Maldives.

Molly captured the stunning welcome bag complete with a bottle of Whispering Angel rose, and posed for an adorable shot with Bambi on their private terrace.

More on Bambi Fury

Mallorca - August 

Baby Bambi jetted on her sixth holiday of the year in August when Molly booked a spur-of-the-moment trip to Spain.

The former Love Island star told her fans how she spontaneously jetted off to Marbella to stay in a £1,000-a-night hotel.

The reality star whisked Bambi off to the exclusive Fendi resort, which is a playground for the rich and famous and has 24-hour VIP service, and a luxury suite with sea views.

Ibiza - July 

The young family headed to Ibiza in July, and it was a very special trip indeed as the boxer popped the question during their stay.

Disneyland Paris - July

Molly-Mae revealed her "heart exploded" as she captured the moment her daughter Bambi went on her first ride at Disneyland Paris.

Greece - June

Molly showed off her enviable figure on a luxury boat in Greece, as she enjoyed another holiday with her girl.

The Love Island star appeared to be staying at the fancy Four Seasons Hotel in Athens in one of their phenomenal Sea View Bungalow rooms.

One night in the rooms can cost up to £2,000 and comes with breathtaking views of the Aegean Sea.

Barbados - June

The Fury family jetted to Barbados, for the wedding of Molly-Mae’s manager Fran Britton.

During the trip, Molly-Mae wowed parents by using a savvy gadget to help baby Bambi keep calm.

Molly showed off her incredible post-baby body in a white bikini - just five months after giving birth.

Dubai - April

In April, Molly-Mae revealed she was taking her daughter on her first holiday to Dubai.

Molly-Mae, Tommy, and Bambi made sure to fly out in style by splashing out on First Class seats for their seven-hour flight.

Baby Bambi appeared to enjoy the trip and was the perfect passenger snuggled in between her mum and dad.

The content creator showed her adorable tot in the £252-per-night Jumeirah Beach Hotel pool.

An enviable designer wardrobe 

Not only has the youngster been on trips that many would dream of, but she’s also got an enviable designer wardrobe too. 

Shortly after her arrival into the world, her influencer mum took to Instagram, sharing a snap of Bambi’s closet.

Packed with a large collection of onesies, dresses, and shoes, it's a wardrobe many would dream of.

Spotted in her drawers were a pair of baby UGG Tasman slippers, which retail at a whopping £40.

Not only this, but on her travels, the famous child was also seen wearing the now sold-out Molo strawberry swimsuit, which was listed on Childrensalon Outlet for £49.

Paired with her cozzie, Bambi also has a cute pair of Havaiana flip flops, which range between £20-£24 a pair.

The nursery of dreams 

If you haven’t seen Bambi’s all-white nursery - firstly, where have you been hiding, but secondly, prepare to be stunned.

Bambi’s £10,000 neutral nursery is one that’s sure to leave many open-mouthed, complete with a £460 cloud chandelier and a £5,000 modern acrylic crib.

It’s been reported that the Love Island pair splashed out £400 on interior design service Neo Home to design the aesthetically stunning nursery. 

Not only is the nursery complete with a beautiful cloud feature, but there’s also a personalised neon sign, which can be found on Not on The High Street for £160. 

As well as this, there’s an Ottie and the Bea Mr Maria Miffy Lamp, which costs £160.

The adorable setting was further brought to life with cosy furnishings such as pom pom cushions for £60 each and ball cushions which can be found on Etsy for £30.39 each. 

A comfortable light beige two seater sofa was placed next to the crib and a similar one can be found on Dusk Sloane for just £369.

As well as this, there’s a designer Hermes Avalon throw blanket, which costs £1,430, which  was draped over the couch, while a modern Wayfair concrete accent table, for £132.99, displayed some books. 

Molly showed off the stunning nursery in a social media post, which was captioned: "The Nursery. It only took me nearly two months to find the time to take pics of Bambi’s room… a part of the house that feels like a little slice of heaven."

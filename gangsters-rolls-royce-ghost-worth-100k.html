A GANGSTER'S Rolls Royce Ghost worth £100,000 is up for sale at auction.

The luxury motor was seized under the Proceeds of Crime Act from the owner, who was at the forefront of a multimillion-pound narcotics ring operating in Essex.

Wilsons Auctions is set to present the flashy 2012 car to enthusiasts and collectors this month.

With under 49,000 miles on the clock, the vehicle will be sold on behalf of a law enforcement agency committed to combating organised crime.

In total, the criminal group have been ordered to repay a hefty £800,000 confiscation order.

Allan McKenzie at Wilsons Auctions said: "This 2012 Rolls Royce Ghost is a stunning example of automotive craftsmanship and an excellent acquisition for any discerning buyer.

"Luxury doesn’t stop at the Rolls Royce – other items listed in the catalogue for this auction include an excellent Patek Philippe Nautilus, multiple Rolex models, designer handbags and a fabulous range of exquisite diamond jewellery."

Other items being auctioned off include a BMW X5, Rolex Sea-Dweller 400, and vintage Chanel handbags.

The Online Luxury Goods Auction will run from 11am on May 26 to May 31.

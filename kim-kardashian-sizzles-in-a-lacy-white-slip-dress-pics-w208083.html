Kim Kardashian Sizzles In a Lacy White Slip Dress: Pics

March 29, 2024
blog

Oh, Lord (Disick)! Kim Kardashian showed off her famous curves in a white lace dress at Scott Disick’s birthday party on Thursday, May 26 — and it was anything but pure. 

The mom of North, 2, and Saint, 5 months, picked a patchwork slip dress by Florence fashion house Ermanno Scervino. The frock featured spaghetti straps, a low-cut neckline and layers of diaphanous, delicate lace. An extra layer of material around her waist shielded her from a wardrobe malfunction, but completely sheer panels right below her bust and around her legs and back showed off good stretches of her skin. Kardashian, 35, accessorized with a matching lace choker and nude ankle-strap sandals.

In a same-day Instagram post that displayed her perfectly pink lips and dramatic smoky eyes, she sent the birthday boy a sweet message. “Happy Birthday Lord!!! Spending all day trying to find the right pic to post made me remember all of our amazing memories as a family!” she captioned.

This isn’t the first time this week that Kardashian has been all over the lace. At the 100th anniversary celebration for British Vogue on Monday, May 23, she stunned in a semi-sheer Cavalli Couture dress designed by Roberto Cavalli’s creative director, Peter Dundas. The gown was studded with glass beads and pearls and was cinched at the waist with a matching corset belt.

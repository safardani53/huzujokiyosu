MALAYSIA Pargo will return in season nine of VH1's hit series Basketball Wives.

The 40-year-old mother of three was married to former NBA player Jannero Pargo.

Who is Malaysia Pargo from Basketball Wives dating now?

Malaysia Pargo has not publicly stated she is dating anyone.

After her split with Pargo, Malaysia was reportedly romantically involved with NBA player James Harden, rapper O.T. Genasis and music producer Jamal Rashid.

In 2016, Malaysia and O.T. Genasis were seen together at multiple events, and even shared pictures of each other on their social media profiles.

However, after her split with O.T. Genasis, the 40-year-old reality star was featured in an episode of Basketball Wives: Los Angeles on a date with sports agent Debonair Ron.

Ron was introduced to Malaysia by co-star Cecilia Scott and her husband Byron Scott.

However, there was no indication whether the pair were ever a couple after their date.

Eager fans can tune into the first episode of Basketball Wives season nine on Tuesday, February 9th on VH1.

BIRMINGHAM CITY are set to bank a huge eight-figure windfall as Jude Bellingham has completed his £115million move to Real Madrid.

The England international has signed for the Spanish giants and become the most expensive British player of all-time.

Bellingham's much-anticipated move has finally been completed and the 19-year-old leaves Borussia Dortmund after three seasons.

The Mirror previously reported that the teenager's former side Birmingham will receive £10million as part of the deal that sees him go to Real Madrid.

The Blues added the clause in when he was sold to the German side in 2020.

It means the Championship side will have now banked £35million from the sale of Bellingham.

RACING takes place at Haydock today and we have you covered with all the latest runners, results, tips and going news.

What time does racing start at Haydock?

The first race is at 1.35pm.

There are eight races on the card and the last race is due off at 5.35pm.

What TV channel is Haydock on?

You can watch the racing at Haydock on Racing UK.

Racing UK is on Sky channel 432, Virgin channel 536, and UPC in Ireland on channel 411.

The 2.05, 2.40 and 3.15 will be shown live on ITV4.

What is the going at the racecourse?

Sean Allen Morley, known by his stage name Val Venis, is a retired Canadian professional wrestler. He is known for his appearances with World Wrestling Entertainment from 1998 to 2009. He has also worked for Consejo Mundial de Lucha Libre and Total Nonstop Action Wrestling. During his WWF / WWE career, Venis has twice hosted the Intercontinental Championship, once European Champion and a World Tag Team Champion with Lance Storm. As a masked Steele, he is the youngest and first non-Mexican to win the CMLL world heavyweight title.

BAD Bunny is one of the biggest artists on the planet, and continues to skyrocket to the top of the music charts.

Since his humble beginnings in Almirante Sur, Puerto Rico, the rapper-singer has taken the reggaetón world by storm.

What is Bad Bunny's real name?

Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio, better known by his stage name Bad Bunny, is a Puerto Rican reggeaton artist.

He has collaborated with artists including Jennifer Lopez, J Balvin, Ozuna, Farruko, El Alfa, Arcángel and Daddy Yankee.

As a child he was shy and described himself to Rolling Stone as being a wallflower, though he developed a reputation at school for coming up with creative raps.

He was raised in the Catholic Church and knew from a young age he wanted to be a singer, so would sing in the choir.

Bad Bunny, 26, has released three studio albums: "X 100pre" (2018), "YHLQMDLG" (2020), "El Ultimo Tour Del Mundo", and a joint album with J Balvin titled "Oasis".

His biggest tracks include "Dakiti", "Yo Perreo Sola", "La Romana", "Ignorantes", and "Tití Me Preguntó".

Where did Bad Bunny get his name from?

His stage name “Bad Bunny” was awarded to him because of a childhood picture that circulated the internet in which he's dressed as a rabbit.

Sporting a not-so-happy face in the picture, he created a Twitter account under the handle Bad Bunny, using the picture as his display pic.

When Bunny gained more notoriety the name stuck.

What is Bad Bunny's net worth?

Bad Bunny is the one of the most streamed artists on Spotify globally.

His latest album "Un Verano Sin Ti", which released in May 2022, made history for being the fastest album to hit 6billion streams on Spotify.

Bad Bunny beat out the likes of Drake, The Weeknd, J Balvin, and late rapper Juice WRLD, to claim the number one spot.

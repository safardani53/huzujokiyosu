'We've had quite a lot of sex' - jockey's unbelievable blunder caught on live TV

May 15, 2024
blog

JOCKEY Callum Shepherd was left red-faced after his side-splitting slip of the tongue was caught on live TV.

The rider was chatting to Sun Racing columnist Matt Chapman at Lingfield when the Sky Sports cameras picked up his hilarious blunder.

Shepherd was describing his partnership with trainer George Scott and the winners they have banged in together.

But he seemed to reference a different sort of riding and banging altogether when he accidentally said: "We've had quite a lot of sex."

Shepherd quickly corrected himself and said: "Quite a lot of success together - that came out a bit wrong."

By this point Chappers was already cracking up and Shepherd could do nothing to suppress his sheepish grin.

Chappers said: "That's going to go viral I'm afraid, Callum."

To which the flustered jockey replied, 'is this live?' before walking off camera.

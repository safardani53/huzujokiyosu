How much is Marcus Rashford's salary at Man Utd and what's his net worth?

MARCUS Rashford MBE is a footballer who uses his platform to campaign against child hunger and racism. 

He is a crucial member of Manchester United, and is a key player in the England national team. 

How much is Marcus Rashford's salary at Manchester United?   

Marcus Rashford has been at Manchester United since he was a child, having signed up to the academy at the age of seven.

He made his debut for the club on February 25, 2016, in a Europa League clash against Danish club Midtjylland - and scored twice.

He then scored a brace in his Premier League debut against Arsenal on February 28, 2016.

He also scored in his first Manchester derby and on his EFL Cup and UEFA Champions League debuts.

He went on to become the youngest English player to score in his first senior international match and was picked for the Euros 2016.

He represented England at the 2018 and 2022 FIFA World Cup. 

His early success led him to sign his first contract with United on May 30, 2016.

The deal saw him earn £20,000 a week, and signed until 2020.

On July 1, 2019, Rashford signed a new four-year contract with the Manchester club worth £41,600,000, which expires in June 2023.

Under the new terms the Red Devils pay Rashford an eye-watering salary of £200,000 a week

His annual average salary is estimated at £10,400,000, according to sportrac, making him the joint seventh highest-paid player at the club. 

What is Marcus Rashford's net worth?    

Rashford was born on October 31, 1997, in Manchester. 

He was raised by his single mother, Melanie Maynard, who worked a number of jobs in order to feed her family of five children.  

Despite his family's difficulties, Rashford’s passion for the beautiful game saw him join the Manchester youth system at only seven years old. 

Despite his immense success, Rashford never forgot his humble beginnings and went on to campaign against child poverty and hunger. 

He became the youngest footballer to become a member of the Most Excellent Order of the British Empire (MBE) at 22 years old. 

As of May 2023, Rashford has a net worth of around £65million.  

Much of his earnings come from his Manchester United salary, sponsorship campaigns and endorsements from McDonald’s, EA Sports and Nike.  

In 2021, he topped The Sunday Times Giving List - a list measuring "the generosity of those financially best equipped to make a difference".

Who are the top earners at Man Utd? 

Manchester United’s top earner is David De Gea.

The goalkeeper is reportedly on £375,000 a week.

Jadon Sancho is next on £350,000, with Raphael Varane earning a cool £340,000 a week.

WITH the best thermal gloves, your kids will be happy to make the most of the parks and playgrounds this winter.

That’s why we’ve rounded up the best pairs to buy now so you're all set for the winter chill.

Thermal gloves are an essential accessory during the colder, winter months as they provide another layer of thick fabric to help keep your child warm.

It’s especially important to protect your child's feet and hands in the snow and wet weather to prevent frostbite, which can be very painful.

Thermal gloves made from wool or fleece will keep little fingers from freezing and you can even buy waterproof gloves if your kids like to get hands-on in the great outdoors.

A high-quality pair of gloves will see your child through at least one winter and give you the peace of mind that they are warm enough to still play outdoors.

We've selected the best thermal gloves for kids that are certain to keep their hands warm and shielded from the elements.

Where to buy the best thermal gloves for children

You can buy thermal gloves from most big high street brands, but we recommend looking at outdoor sites such as Decathlon, Go Outdoors or Mountain Warehouse.

They usually stock a wide range of thermal gloves all of which are designed for children to use on the go so they're flexible and comfortable as well as functional.

Then you can select the right shape, size and functionality such as touchscreen-friendly fingertips to find the best thermal gloves to suit your child's needs.

What are the best thermal gloves for children?

The best thermal gloves for children always have an additional lining, which could be made from thinsulate, fleece or extreme wadding to hold in the heat and provide extra warmth for your child’s chilly hands.

Our personal favourites are the Trekmates mitts as they are extremely well insulated, but still breathable and flexible for your child's movement.

Another important aspect is keeping them clean – we’ve included a few hardwearing pairs on our list that can be chucked into the washing machine as often as is necessary.

KELLY Clarkson has announced another huge career move after she fled Los Angeles to make a major change for her daytime talk show.

Over the summer, the Since U Been Gone singer moved The Kelly Clarkson Show from the Universal Lot in LA to Studio 6A in 30 Rock.

Kelly, 41, took to Instagram to announce her new XM channel, Kelly Clarkson Connection, to her social media followers.

The post, which included a promo photo of the daytime talk show host smiling, was captioned: "I’m inviting you to enjoy my new channel ‘Kelly Clarkson Connection’ with the @SiriusXM Listen Free Event now through Dec 4 at Ch 12.

"It’s already on, just turn the radio on in your car to listen for free: https://www.siriusxm.com/KellyClarksonLF."

In the post's comment section, one person wrote: "Wow! You are amazing!!!"

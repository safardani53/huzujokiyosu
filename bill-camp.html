Who is Bill Camp:

Bill Camp is a famous Movie Actor. He was born on October 13, 1961 and his birthplace is Massachusetts. Bill is also well known as, Emmy nominated actor for his work in the HBO limited series The Night Of who also earned a long list of film credits. He has been featured in films such as Rounders (1998), Lawless (2012), Lincoln (2012), 12 Years a Slave (2013), Birdman (2014), Aloha (2015), Black Mass (2015), Vice (2018) and Joker (2019).

Bill is originated from United States. He was the detective trying to figure out the case against Riz Ahmed’s Naz Khan in The Night Of.

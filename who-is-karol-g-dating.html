Who is Karol G dating?

KAROL G delighted fans when she appeared to confirm a new romance at her March 2023 concert in Puerto Rico.

The Colombian singer's relationship status has been the focus of much speculation since her split with rapper Anuel AA in 2019.

Who is Karol G dating?

Karol G was formerly engaged to Puerto Rican rapper, Anuel AA, in 2019, but the pair confirmed their split in 2021.

Following the split in April 2021, Karol G wrote on her Instagram story: "If just accepting it is hard enough, imagine having to do it in front of millions of people...

"For a long time we tried to keep our relationship away from social media in order to protect ourselves.

"We never used our relationship for marketing purposes, not today, not even almost three years ago when we started [dating]."

The couple appeared to end the relationship on a friendly note with Anuel stating that the couple will forever be great friends.

Since the split, the singer has been reportedly dating Colombian singer and songwriter Feid.

After months of fan speculation, the two appeared to confirm rumors of a romance when they appeared together on stage.

Wearemitu.com reports that Karol G brought Feid onstage as a "very special guest" during her concert at San Juan’s Hiram Bithorn stadium in March 2023.

Fans have reportedly been making social media accounts dedicated to the duo's possible relationship.

Who is Karol G?

Carolina Giraldo Navarro - professionally known as Karol G - is a singer and songwriter. She was born on February 14, 1991, and raised in Medellin, Colombia.

The singer is described as a reggaeton and Latin trap artist but has also experimented with other genres including reggae and sertanejo.

Aged 14, Karol G competed on the Colombian version of The X Factor but failed to win.

She has over 65million followers on Instagram, and is heavily inspired by Rihanna and the late Selena Quintanilla - she even has a tattoo of their faces next to her own on her forearm.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Karol G has an estimated net worth of around $25million.

What are Karol G's most popular songs?

In 2017, the rapper collaborated with Puerto Rican rapper Bad Bunny, and Ahora Me Llama became her breakthrough hit.

It was the lead single for her debut album Unstoppable, also released in 2017.

Her song Secreto became a hit in late 2018, in Latin America, and the rapper publicly confirmed her relationship with Anuel AA in her music video.

In 2019 she released a collaboration with Anuel AA, Daddy Yankee, Ozuna, and J Balvin, titled China, which became the artist's first music video with over one billion views on YouTube.

Her song Tusa, with Nicki Minaj, was certified 28x Latin Platinum by the RIAA. It charted internationally and stayed on the Billboard Hot Latin Songs chart for 25 weeks.

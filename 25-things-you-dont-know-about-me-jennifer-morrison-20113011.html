25 Things You Don't Know About Me: Jennifer Morrison

July 18, 2024
blog

Jennifer Morrison, 32, shares the 25 things you don't know about her with Us Weekly.

1. I am extremely punctual, yet I turned this list in late.

2. My favorite vacation location is Lake Como in Italy.

3. My dog Ava is my first and favorite pet.

4. Yoga probably makes me a better person.

5. Red wine also probably makes me a better person.

6. My favorite movie ever is Bicycle Thieves.

7. My favorite feel-good movie is Ferris Bueller's Day Off.

8. I recently discovered listening to music on vinyl.

9. Nothing makes me crabbier than being cold and wet — especially at the same time.

10. I still cannot watch Chucky movies.

11. My favorite book is a tie between The Great Gatsby and East of Eden.

12. I have a sister, Julie, and a brother, Dan. Both have jobs involving music.

13. I played clarinet and piano in high school.

14. My costar Ginnifer Goodwin gave me a custom copy of the complete works of Grimm's Fairy Tales for my birthday five years ago!

15. My favorite color changes every day.

16. I am obsessed with HGTV. I love me some Holmes on Homes!

17. My favorite restaurant in Vancouver [where Once Upon a Time films] is the Italian Kitchen.

18. My favorite restaurant in L.A. is a tie between Madeo and Pace.

19. I am a terrible speller.

20. I love, love, love The Good Wife.

21. I got my SAG card when I was 10 by starring in a Beenie Weenie’s commercial.

22. I have seven scars from having moles removed. One was a melanoma, six were precancerous. Get your moles checked!

23. I've had about 35 Disney vacations. My parents are obsessed.

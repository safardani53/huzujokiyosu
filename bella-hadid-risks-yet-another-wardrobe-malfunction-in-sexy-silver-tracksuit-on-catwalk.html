Bella Hadid risks yet ANOTHER wardrobe malfunction in sexy silver tracksuit on catwalk The Sun

August 16, 2024
blog

BELLA Hadid risked another wardrobe malfunction as she took to the catwalk in a revealing silver tracksuit on Thursday evening.

The 19-year-old made sure all eyes were on her during amfAR's 23rd Cinema Against AIDS Gala at Hotel du Cap-Eden-Roc in Cannes, France as she wore a silver jacket and matching slit trousers, with no bra underneath.

As Bella strutted her stuff on the runway alongside fellow beauties Barbara Palvin, Izabel Goulart and Lara Stone , her ample cleavage was on full display with just the high-collared jacket carefully stuck in place to cover her modesty.

The garment also featured slashed arms to match the trouser legs, putting a disco twist on the sports luxe style.

The ensemble was precariously held in place by strips of tape, which were visible on Bella's skin as she moved around.

As well as putting her bust on display, Bella also showcased her slender pins and flat tummy.

Her metallic look was completed with a pair of silver strappy sandals.

The beauty will have been keen to not have a repeat performance of the unfortunate wardrobe malfunction which occured on the red carpet at the Cannes Film Festival on Wednesday night.

The fashion darling wowed in a slinky halterneck, backless red gown by Alexandre Vauthier which featured a low cut front and a hip high slit.

However Bella ended up showing a little more than intended as she climbed the steps to the screening of Belgian drama La Fille Inconnue (The Unknown Girl).

As the made her way up, the split of her dress separated too far, revealing a barely-there matching thong underneath.

She completed her barely there outfit with a pair of strappy black Giuseppe Zanotti stilettos, diamond bracelet and matching teardrop earrings.

Bella has definitely been stepping out of her older sister and fellow supermodel Gigi's shadow in recent months, having stripped off for a recent fashion spread in the Spanish edition of Harper's Bazaar magazine.

In one cheeky shot, Bella can be seen baring some saucy underboob, and another picture shows her topless as she covers her breasts with her arms.

Bella expressed her pride over the high-profile magazine spread by sharing the pictures on Instagram.

She captioned one of the pictures: "New Harper's Bazaar Spain cover!! Thank You @harpersbazaares."

Bella added in another message: "My cover shoot for @harpersbazaares was put together with two stories. We had one shoot in Paris and one in NYC. This is by @txemayeste in Paris."

12 Best Work Jackets For Women 2021 | The Sun UK

July 03, 2024
blog

IF there's one item of clothing that can instantly pull a smart work look together, it's a well-fitting, perfectly structured jacket.

Many of us spend so much time focusing on a capsule collection of dresses, trousers and blouses for work, that outerwear can almost become a second thought—but the right jacket can truly elevate your office style and create a polished and professional aesthetic.

The best work jackets can be worn as separates or as part of a suit if you really mean business. Double-breasted blazers, belted jackets, checks, houndstooth, pastels, bold colours: there are so many ways you can go, so make sure you have a good selection of styles that can be worn on rotation.

Looking to this season's trends—metallics, lilacs and toffees are the biggest colour stories, while capes, ponchos, ruffles and fringing are all having a moment.

Whether you're looking for something ontrend for AW20 or a classic jacket you can wear season after season, we've pulled together a must-have selection that will boost your attire now workplaces are opening again—or perhaps add some polish to your Zoom meeting ensemble.

Meet your new work jacket heroes below.

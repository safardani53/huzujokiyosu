Who is The Bachelor?

Zach Shallcross is the latest bachelor, having been previously introduced to Bachelor Nation during season 19 of The Bachelorette.

In 2022, Shallcross made it to the final three of Gabby Windey and Rachel Recchia's historic co-star run.

The 26-year-old is a 2019 graduate of California Polytechnic State University, where he earned a degree in business administration.

Prior to joining The Bachelor, Shallcross was a sales account executive for a software company named Oracle, located in Austin, Texas.

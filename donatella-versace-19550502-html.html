Donatella Francesca Versace is an Italian fashion designer, businesswoman, socialite, and model. She is the sister of Gianni Versace, founder of the luxury fashion company Versace, of which she inherited a portion upon her brother's death in 1997. She sold the company to Capri Holdings in 2018, but remained its chief creative officer.

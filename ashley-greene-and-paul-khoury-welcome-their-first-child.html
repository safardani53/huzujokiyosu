Greene announced her pregnancy in March of this year. She shared a photo of herself and her spouse holding a sonogram. Greene’s publicist confirmed at the time that the couple was thrilled to be expecting their first child.

Ashley Greene’s age, career, and other information

Ashley Greene was born on February 21, 1987, and is 35 years old. The native Floridian grew up in Middleburg and Jacksonville. Before attending Wolfson High School, she attended University Christian School. Greene moved to Los Angeles, California, when she was 17 years old. She wanted to be a model, but her height prevented her from doing so, so she started working in advertisements. Following that, she became interested in acting.

Ashley first appeared as a guest on TV series such as Punk’d and Crossing Jordan. She later rose to prominence for her part as Alice Cullen in The Twilight Saga film series, which she performed in all five installments. She later played Michelle Burkham in the drama Skateland. She co-starred with Twilight co-star Kellan Lutz in the 2011 romantic sports drama film A Warrior Heart. Summer Blood, a 2009 horror film, and The Apparition, a 2012 supernatural horror film, followed. She then starred in the biographical drama film CBGB in 2013. The Retirement Plan and Some Other Woman are two of her future projects.

Ashley Greene and Paul Khoury’s relationship

Ashley Greene and Paul Khoury got engaged in December 2016, despite the fact that it is unclear how they met. They married in July 2018 in San Jose, California. According to a source, the ceremony was gorgeous, and the pair played rock-paper-scissors to pick who would recite the vows first. For their honeymoon, the couple traveled to Hawaii. Greene suggested having children since she was already planning to increase her family. People quoted her as saying,

“My spouse and I are both thinking, ‘It’s going to happen.'” And believe me, we’ve reached the stage where both of our parents are hinting and saying, ‘OK, we’re ready.’ You’ve been married for a few years now.'”

Greene’s spouse is a TV personality and voice actor. He has also performed on Australian television programs as the bass guitarist with the band Gravel.

Who are William Hurt's children?

William Hurt, aged 71, was married to Mary Beth Hurt from 1971 to 1982, but the pair did not have any children.

Hurt had a total of four children: one with Sandra Jennings, two with Heidi Henderson, and one with French actress, film director and screenwriter, Sandrine Bonnaire.

Hurt and Jennings welcomed son, Alexander Devon Hurt, 39, in 1983.

Hurt and Henderson welcomed their two sons, William, 31, and Samual, 32, in 1991 and 1989, respectively.

Hurt and Sandrine welcomed their daughter, Jeanne, 28, in 1994.

What was his cause of death?

Hurt died at age 71 due to natural causes, according to his son, William.

The Oscar-winning legend known for the 1985 film Kiss Of The Spider Woman passed away Sunday, March 13, 2022.

"It is with great sadness that the Hurt family mourns the passing of William Hurt, beloved father and Oscar winning actor, on March 13, 2022, one week before his 72nd birthday," said a statement by William.

"He died peacefully, among family, of natural causes. The family requests privacy at this time."

Who was William Hurt?

William Hurt was an actor born on March 20, 1950.

During his childhood, Hurt was the Vice President of the Drama Club at Middlesex School in Massachusetts.

He briefly attended Tufts University to study theology, soon after transferring to Juilliard School to pursue acting.

After leaving Juilliard, Hurt took to the stage professionally in the 1970s.

He made his film debut in the 1980 sci-fi, Altered States.

Hurt was nominated for three consecutive Academy Awards for Best Actor in the 1980s, winning for his work on Kiss of the Spider Woman (1985).

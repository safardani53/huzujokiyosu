KATE Gosselin has revealed her shocking new career move alongside Jamie Lynn Spears, three years after she was axed from TLC.

JON VS. KATE

Jon and Kate have been involved in a nasty custody battle after they divorced in 2009 after about 10 years of marriage.

Meanwhile, the exes' two eldest kids Mady and Cara, both 21, were left out of the custody agreements because they are both legal adults and have headed off to college.

Alan Titchmarsh says his novels arent as saucy now hes an OAP

blog

ALAN Titchmarsh is toning down his racy novels because he’s getting older and has calmed down. 

The TV gardener, 71, shocked fans by penning steamy sex scenes in his books but he claims they shouldn’t have been surprised he knows about “pollination”. 

In an exclusive chat with The Sun, he said: “I’ve written 11 novels altogether, perhaps they’re not as racy as when I started. Maybe it’s because I’ve got older and less racy myself. I don’t know. 

“They’re love stories. Romantic adventures is what they are really. Sometimes you’ve got to show how attracted people are to one another.”

The author added he was left red-faced reading out the sex scenes for his audiobooks. 

During lockdown, Alan has been able to work with his wife of 45 years, Alison, for the first time on his Grow Your Own series. 

He said: “She’s not done so much as take a snap on her mobile phone and really she doesn’t take photos at all. And there she was wielding a camera and filming television programmes. I think she was quietly chuffed with herself. I was certainly chuffed with her. She did really well.”

The green-fingered star added that she now critiques television programmes for their techniques and prepared a speech in case they won a TV Bafta.

Alison might yet have a shot at the accolade as Alan is still being offered and turning down work.

He said: “I’m quite picky, not least because I think people can have too much of you. I like to do things that are stimulating, that are challenging, rather than just ploughing the same old furrow. But at the same time, I don’t want to embarrass people. 

“You’re not going to be loved by everybody, some people say ‘oh not him again’. But you can’t really think about them but I am aware of the fact of not overdoing it. 

“Not being all things to all men all the time. You’ve got to pace yourself a bit. Just keep doing it well and I do love what I do.”

Supporting Kendall Jenner Hollywood Life

August 05, 2024

Gigi Hadid, 21, and Zayn Malik, 23, are always picture perfect. But the pair looked especially cute when they attended the Givenchy runway presentation during the label’s Paris Fashion Week Womenswear Spring/Summer 2017, on Oct. 2. Gigi was obviously there to support her squad member, Kendall Jenner, 20, who was walking in the show. And Zayn, being the ever supportive boyfriend came along for the ride!

The dynamic duo looked gorgeous in their respective outfits, with the drop-dead gorgeous model sporting a geometric-patterned, taupe dress and tan knee-high, high-heeled boots. Gigi wore her hair half up and half down, and completed her ensemble by tying a white jacket around her waist. The day before she came to watch her BFF in this show, she hit the catwalk in both the Elie Saab and Mugler shows. So this was probably a much needed break from the actual runway for Gigi, who must have enjoyed being an onlooker who got to cheer for her friend.

Zayn went for a more casual look, with a black T-shirt with a gold circle print in the center, black pants and black dress shoes. He wore a thin gold chain around his neck with a small pendant, but of course, his best accessory was his famous tattoo sleeves. We absolutely love seeing these two together any chance we get and this time was no different. We hope they are enjoying Paris Fashion Week as much as we are enjoying seeing pics of them at the shows!

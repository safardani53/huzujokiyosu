Sculptra — The Newest & Best Filler

Dr. Katz explains the revolutionary new treatment:

Sculptra is a new volumizer. It fills in the areas where people lose volume in their face. It’s actually a lactic acid derivative and it’s used for people with deep loses of volume — for example, if their cheeks are sunken in. It’s great for people who are developing jowls. It’s very safe and has no side effects. Usually we do one treatment, wait a month, and do a second treatment. It’s rare but sometimes people need a third treatment. It stimulates new collagen where people become hollow. You can use it all over face and chin and neck.

When Dr. Katz means no downtime, he means no downtime — it’s literally a lunchtime procedure!

“There is no downtime — you can go right back to work. It’s great for any age group!”

Sculptra costs between $900 and $1200 per treatment, which is comparable to older, traditional fillers like Juvederm.

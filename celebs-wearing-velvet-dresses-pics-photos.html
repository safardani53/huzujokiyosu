The holiday season is here, and velvet is the perfect fabric to wear to your next Christmas event – just like these stars!

Feeling plush! Velvet is a timeless winter trend, perfectly fit for all the festivities of the holiday season. Cozy, luxurious, and gorgeous in every single color, the material is a go-to for fashionistas with a calendar packed with winter parties. Celebrities love velvet on the red carpet too, often strutting around in soft, shiny silhouettes at premieres and awards shows.

As celebrity stylist Rachel Zoe told Bustle in Dec. 2021, “You can pretty much be sure that my holiday attire has always been covered in velvet, sequins, metallics, and beautiful fall colors like deep bordeauxs. I always love to do a great sequin top, cocktail dresses, and jumpsuits.”

Need some inspiration before your next holiday bash? Look no further than our famous friends.

From Selena Gomez to Kim Kardashian to Olivia Culpo, Kendall Jenner, and plenty more, keep clicking through the gallery to see celebrities that slayed the velvet trend.

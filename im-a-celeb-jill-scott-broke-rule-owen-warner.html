I'm A Celeb's Jill Scott admits she broke a strict rule with Owen Warner

May 16, 2024
blog

JILL Scott admitted to breaking camp rules to help out Owen Warner with his hunger in I'm A Celebrity.

The former Lioness admitted she was more than happy to assist the Hollyoaks actor in camp with extra rations.

Jill, 35, who was recently crowned Queen of the Jungle admitted that she would sneak her fellow campmate more food when he was at a low moment.

Speaking to the Mirror, Jill admitted that the pair were very close in I'm A Celebrity, as she said: “He felt like my little brother in there.

"I was always trying to give him an extra spoonful of rice.”

The former footballer also admitted that she and Owen, 23, struck up an instant connection in camp.

The star revealed: "From the first day when I said, ‘You’re on Hollyoaks’ and he said, ‘You play football don’t you?’ I think from then we just had that connection.”

But Jill also admitted that there was a real sense of teamwork in camp as the stars focussed on keeping morale high.

The star explained: "When we all put on that uniform, it was all one team.

"The aim of the whole show was we needed to get stars to provide for the rest of the celebrities."

This year's series of I'm A Celebrity was particularly notable for the inclusion of disgraced former Health Secretary, Matt Hancock.

The Suffolk MP caused an uproar when he signed on to enter camp, after he was previously exposed for his affair with political aide Gina Coladengelo during the pandemic.

But despite this, Jill admitted that she stays out of politics, so she and Owen took the time to get to know Matt in the jungle away from his Westminster persona.

She admitted: “Going into it, my knowledge of politics isn’t high or anything so me and Owen were probably asking a lot of questions and learning things for the first time.

“But in terms of being a campmate and providing for the camp and bringing back stars, I thought he did a good job.”

This comes after it was revealed that Jill will be going back to her regular day job next week.

Despite winning millions of votes from the public, the star will return to serving customers at the Boxx2Boxx coffee shop in Manchester.

The star co-owns the casual hot drink establishment in Northenden, Manchester with her fiancé Shelly Unitt.

She told The Sun that despite her newfound fame in the jungle, she is still insistent on going back to work.

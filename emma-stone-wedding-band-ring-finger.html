Emma Stone flashes wedding band during video chat with Reese Witherspoon about anxiety

September 13, 2024
blog

EMMA Stone has hinted that she's secretly tied the knot with fiance Dave McCary.

The 31-year-old Oscar-winner flashed a gold wedding band on her ring finger, as she was interviewed by Reese Witherspoon.

The La La Land star sported the band - which had replaced her diamond engagement ring - during a chat featured on Reese Witherspoon's Hello Sunshine You Tube channel on Tuesday.

She made further reference when asked by Dr. Harold Koplewicz, also on the video chat, who was discussing anxiety.

"If you marry an anxious man, you're going to have to know me the rest of my life," said Dr Koplewicz.

"Thankfully I didn't do that," laughed Stone.

Emma was thought to have postponed her wedding to McCary amid the coronavirus pandemic.

They had been set to tie the knot in March in Los Angeles, but Page Six reported the wedding was cancelled.

Large gatherings were not permitted under California's stay at home order, however, it's possible they had an intimate legal ceremony.

The pair announced their engagement in December after two years of dating.

Stone first met McCary, a former segment director on SNL, when she hosted the show in 2016.

They were first romantically linked in October 2017.

Dave, 34, confirmed he had popped the question to the Amazing Spider-Man star with a sweet Instagram post, which saw Emma, 31, flashing her new bling, last December.

Emma previously dated her Spider-Man co-star Andrew Garfield, from 2011 to 2015 and they stayed close friends.

The Sun has reached out to Emma's reps for comment.

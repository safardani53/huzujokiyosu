TEEN Mom Farrah Abraham's rarely-seen sister Ashley Danielsen resurfaced in their mom Deb's new video.

Unlike her mom and sister, Ashley has maintained a fairly private life.

In a new YouTube video shared by Deb on Monday, the Teen Mom OG star shared how she spent her Mother's Day surrounded by her loved ones.

The aspiring musician first reunited with Farrah, 30, and Sophia as they picked up Debra's mom for dinner.

At dinner, they met up with Farrah's sister, Ashley, marking it four generations of the family.

Ashley specifically stood out with her dark hair and tan skin as she cut her daughter Sylvia's food.

"So, we are celebrating four generations today and this is what we are doing," Debra could be heard saying as she moved the camera over each family member.

"Here's Gigi, great-grandma, my mom. Then there's Farrah, Ashley, Sophia, Sylvia (Ashley's daughter), and Axel (Sylvia's son)."

She continued as she moved the camera towards her side: "David and then me! So happy Mother's Day, everybody!"

Unlike Ashley, Debra and Farrah love being in the limelight.

Sadly, this hasn't made motherhood any easier for Farrah as she recently admitted to having "frustrating and depressing" days as a single mom while visiting her late baby daddy’s grave.

Ahead of the celebration, the reality star visited the grave of Derek Underwood along with her daughter, Sophia.

Because he was born on May 8th, Farrah and Sophia decorated Derek's headstone with Happy Birthday balloons and signs.  

In the video, Farrah spoke about her former partner, who died when he was 18, and said: "He was not only a gift to a mother at one time, but he is a reminder he gave me one of the biggest gifts of my lifetime."

The Teen Mom star described herself as a mother who has "lonely days, sad days," on top of "frustrating" days. 

Back in 2008, Derek passed away from a car accident.

On December 28th, Farrah's former partner allegedly lost control of his car while he was driving.

The car skidded and flipped over onto a power pole, which killed Derek and passenger Zachary Mendoza.

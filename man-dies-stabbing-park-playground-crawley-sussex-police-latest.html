Man dies after being 'stabbed at children's play park' in broad daylight - as cops arrest 22-year-ol

August 17, 2024
blog

A MAN has died after being stabbed at a children's play park in Crawley.

Sussex Police have arrested a 22-year-old man following the attack at around 10.30am this morning.

Officers were called to Cherry Lane Adventure Playground in the town following reports of a stabbing.

Paramedics battled to save the victim, in his 60s, but he was pronounced dead a short time later.

The scene was cordoned off by police and forensics officers are currently combing the area for evidence.

The 22-year-old man remains in police custody having been arrested nearby on suspicion of attempted murder shortly after the attack.

Residents living near the scene of the attack were tonight perplexed over the killing.

Neighbours living adjacent to Cherry Lane in Crawley, described the stabbing as a "one-off occurrence" but did not know what had happened.

One neighbour said: "I don't know anything other than a 22-year-old has been arrested.

"This is definitely a one-off occurrence. We don't get police cordons here much."

Police are appealing for witnesses and have asked anyone with information to come forward.

Anyone with any information is asked to call Sussex Police on 101 quoting crime number 461 of 28/11.

Alternatively, you can visit the Crimestoppers website or contact the independent charity anonymously on 0800 555 111.

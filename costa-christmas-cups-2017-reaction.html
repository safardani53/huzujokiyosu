Costa has released their Christmas cup designs of the year... and they've received a warmer receptio

July 25, 2024
blog

COSTA has marked the countdown to Christmas with a collection of limited edition drinks.

And each year, the coffee giant impresses by rolling out specially-designed drinks vessels.

As the illustrated cups are so hyped up, the cafe chain has very high expectations to live up to.

But thankfully, early social media reactions suggest that Costa has smashed it with their 2017 creations.

Customers will be handed winter drinks holders adorned with five different designs.

A ginger bread man, snowman, elf, drummer boy and Santa Claus are among the illustrations on offer.

Coffee lovers are already excited to get their hands on the new winter drinks holders.

One Twitter user said: "Is it really sad that I'm looking forward to tomorrow because it's time for Costa's Christmas cups?"

Another added: "The Costa Christmas cups this year are so cute."

And a third agreed: "Can't wait for the Costa & Starbucks Christmas cups."

The festive menu is comprised of a number of different drinks.

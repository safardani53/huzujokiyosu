Angel Haze: Her Racy Sex Life With Ireland Baldwin Revealed

Angel says she’s sick of people assuming her months-long girlfriend, Ireland, is just another one of her pals. 

“I don’t know if there’s like some confirm or deny thing with the way relationships work in the media, but everyone just calls us best friends, best friends for life, like we’re just friends hanging out. It’s funny. It’s rad in some ways, it sucks in others,” Angel opens up to UK newspaper The Independent. Angel says she believes people just assume they’re BFFs because they’re girls. 

“There are still certain limitations for women. If we were two guys, it’d be insane, negatively insane with the attention. With us it’s all being very positive, the media are like, ‘Oh they’re so cute, they’re best friends,’” she continues. 

Angel then spills — in a not-so-subtle way — what exactly keeps the two from being just “friends.”

“An interracial gay couple, I mean that’s just weird for America right now. We f**k and friends don’t f**k. I have never f**ked one of my friends. Once I see you in that way, it doesn’t happen,” she says. “But we do f**k and it’s crazy and that’s weird to say because I think about it in terms of an audience reading it and them thinking, ‘What the hell?’ But it happens,” she tells the paper.

Well, alright then. That’s one way to confirm a relationship! We applaud Angel for being so open about her thoughts on interracial relationships and gender stereotypes — but maybe she could’ve explained that they’re a romantic couple without the f**ks?

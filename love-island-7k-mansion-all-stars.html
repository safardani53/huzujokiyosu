LOVE Island has splashed out on £7k a DAY to hire an incredible eco mansion with a zip line and Olympic sized pool for the upcoming All Stars series.

The ITV spin-off, which will be hosted by Maya Jama, will hit our screens on January 15.

Fans of the popular reality dating show will see a number of former Islanders return in a bid to find love again.

Love Island All Stars will take place in Franschhoek, Western Cape, following last year's winter version in South Africa in the same villa.

The sexy singletons will call the incredible mansion - Ludus Magnus - home for the next few weeks - which was previously rented out to Love Island producers for £7k a day.

The lavish villa, which is located in the gorgeous Cape Winelands District Municipality, is eco friendly with 266 solar panels.

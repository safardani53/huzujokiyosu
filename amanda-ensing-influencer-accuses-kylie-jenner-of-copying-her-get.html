Influencer Accuses Kylie Jenner of Copying Her, Gets Shut Down Hard!

April 01, 2024
blog

If you follow Kylie Jenner on Instagram, then you are well aware that she is on yet another boujie vacay with her friends. 

And that her nude photoshoots from the trip have been getting all the attention.

Some of that attention, however, has not been so gracious. 

After a week of Kylie posting the most seductive photos from her tropical trip to Turks and Caicos, an Instagram influencer whose name is Amanda Ensing was not a fan. 

Ensing quickly took to the comment section to drag on one of Kylie’s photos which showed her celebrating her new Kylie Skin collection. 

"Vacation Mode," Jenner captioned the post, as seen below. 

The cute pic showed the reality star sitting on the ledge of a pool wearing nothing but a giant straw hat that covered her face.

She used her hands and legs to cover up, of course, so the 21-year-old mom wasn’t completely exposed! 

While most followers commented uplifting and kind messages, saying how stunning she looked, Amanda decided to diss the young billionaire instead.

Because haters gonna hate… 

"This photo looks awfully familiar," Ensing stated in reference to her June 17th post.

Below is Ensing’s pose… (which ok is pretty similar, even down to the anklet, but who cares?!) 

And while Kylie is usually one to ignore rude comments, because she has way more important things to do with her life, she couldn’t let that diss slide.

"From the words of Kim K, ur not on my mood board but I did get my inspo off Pinterest," she fired back. 

Shut. Down.

Let’s all take a moment to slip this clapback safely into our pockets… that’s quality stuff right there.  

This isn’t the first time Kylie has felt the need to defend herself and set the record straight though.

Earlier this summer Alex Rodriguez also came in hot with a stab at the businesswoman.

"Kylie was talking about Instagram and her lipstick, and how rich she is," the former MLB player said of Kylie at the 2019 Met Gala.

Kylie, however, remembered their conversation much differently.

"Umm no i didn’t," the reality TV star fired back on Twitter.

"We only spoke about Game of Thrones." 

Rodriguez replied, obviously trying to fix things with the young Kardashian sister.

"OMG that’s right @ KylieJenner. It was me talking about you and your makeup line and how much my girls love you."

Awkward…

As for Kylie’s most recent beach getaway, close friends including Scott Disick’s GF Sofia Richie, Draya Michele, Stassi Karanikolaou and baby Stormi Webster were all in attendance. 

"Let the adventures begin!! #KylieSkinSummerTrip," the E! star shared to Instagram earlier this week.

"Hello Paradise!"

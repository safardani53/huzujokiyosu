Bella Thorne &amp; Charlie Puth: Dating!

September 03, 2024
blog

It seems like it was just three months ago that we reported that Bella Thorne is dating Tyler Posey.

The reason for that, of course, is that it was just three months ago.

But while they seemed pretty heavily into one another for at least a few weeks there, it seems Bella and Tyler are officially dunz-o, and the actress/social media celeb has wasted no time in finding someone new.

Yes, it seems that Bella is dating Charlie Puth.

The couple was first spotted together at the final show of the 2016 iHeartRadio Jingle Ball Tour in LA.

They were very friendly on the red carpet, but most outlets who covered the event were hesitant to declare them an official couple.

The next day, Bella and Charlie were spotted making out and holding hands while strolling on the beach.

So yeah, there still hasn’t been any sort of confirmation from either party, but we’re gonna assume that these two are more than just friends with benefits.

But maybe not … 

As you may recall, Puth was hooking up with Selena Gomez for a while, but the two of them parted ways before a real relationship developed.

So maybe Charlie is some kind of new up-and-coming player.

And Bella seems to have no trouble ditching one dude and moving on to the next one, so he may have met his match.

Prior to Posey, Bella dated Gregg Sulkin for over a year, but when the relationship went south, she moved on with the quickness, as one should at 19.

We’re sure she’ll end up taking some flak for the speed with which she moved on this time, (Posey stars on MTV’s Teen Wolf, which has a fanatical online following of very passionate fans.), but it seems to us that the girl is just enjoying her youth.

And besides ,she’s still about a dozen famous dudes shy of tying the record set by the Queen of High-Profile Romances, Taylor Swift.

Plus, with Bella, we’re not subjected to a new breakup song every time one of her relationships comes to an end. Bonus.

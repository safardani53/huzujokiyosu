Who is Leela James:

Leela James is a famous R&B Singer. She was born on June 2, 1983 and her birthplace is Los Angeles, CA. Leela is also well known as, R&B vocalist and songwriter known for such award-nominated albums as Let’s Do It Again and Fall For You. Her most popular album, My Soul, peaked at number seven on the United States’ R&B music chart. She also appeared on seasons two and three of R&B Divas.

Leela is originated from United States. She opened for Macy Gray during the singer’s 2004 concert tour.

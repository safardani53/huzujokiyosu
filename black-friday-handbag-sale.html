When is Black Friday 2021?

On November 26, Black Friday will officially hit the high street and online again - promising massive discounts on all of your favourite fashion stores.

Many brands including designer faves like Mulberry get in there early, saving shoppers the stress of staying up till midnight to bag the best deals. Because of that, it's always best to keep your eyes peeled for Black Friday handbag sale deals from early November onwards.

And when it comes to bagging the best bargain, make sure you shop around on Black Friday sales, as the same products are often on sale with multiple retailers - at varying prices.

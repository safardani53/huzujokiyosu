Heather Locklear Early Life

Born as Heather Deen Locklear in Westwood, Los Angeles, California, the United States of America to Diane Locklear and Bill Locklear, Heather was raised in an upper-class family. She is an American and is of Native American descent.

After graduation from Newbury Park High School, Heather joined the University of California. There she began her modeling career and she was also seen in commercials of the school store.

Love Affairs, Marriage, Boyfriends

Heather was once in love affairs with Scott Baio. She then began dating Mötley Crüe drummer Tommy Lee and finally got married to him on the 10th of May 1986. She remained his loving spouse for about half a decade and then got divorced on the 16th of August 1993.

On the 17th of October 1994, Heather got married for the second time to Bon Jovi guitarist Richie Sambora. As a married couple, they welcomed their first child together in 1997.

They got divorced on the 11th of April 2007. She also got engaged with Jack Wagner but called off her engagement within a few months.

Acting Career & Net Worth

She made her professional acting debut in 1980 and that was in the television series, CHiPs. She finally reached her eminence as an actress after she appeared in the television series, Melrose Place.

For her role in this television series, she earned a salary of $100,000 /episode. Her net worth is rumored to be around $25 million.

DINERS will have to act quickly to get their hands on a fan favorite that's only back for a limited time.

Sonic has brought back the Grilled Cheese Double Burger as part of a new combo.

The burger features two seasoned junior beef patties layered with three slices of American cheese, ketchup, mustard, and diced onions all between two thick pieces of Texas Toast.

Customers have been waiting for the sandwich to make a comeback since it was initially introduced in 2021.

They're so good that the first time the burgers returned to the menu one Twitter user wrote: "I take back everything I said about how bad this day was.

"The grilled cheeseburger from Sonic is back. I have found peace again."

A DELICIOUS DEAL

This time around, the Grilled Cheese Burger is part of a combo meal that costs just $3.99.

The money-saving deal comes with the burger and a choice of small tater tots or small fires.

Diners can order the meal in person and through the chain's app or website, but they'll have to act fast.

The combo is only available for a limited time at participating locations.

ANOTHER NEW OPTION

Sonic has been on a roll updating its menu.

Earlier this week, the restaurant introduced the Strawberry Shortcake Snowball Slush Float.

The drink consists of real strawberries, ice cream, and the signature Sonic Slush.

Plus, it's topped with vanilla ice cream and sugar crystals for the perfect finish.

Mackenzie Gibson, vice president of culinary and menu innovation at Sonic, says the new drink “provides a delicious respite from the summer heat” that customers will appreciate.

“This Snowball Slush Float is the perfect summertime treat to sip on while road-tripping, heading to the beach, or barbecuing with family,” she said in a press release.

Plus, Sonic die-hards can get their hands on some new merchandise starting on June 1.

The company created a line of beach and pool products that are perfect for summer vacation, including:

  • a pool float in the shape of a cherry limeade drink
  • a cabana set featuring a Sonic pattern
  • retro cherry red sunglasses
  • a sonic beach towel, cooler bag, and summer tumbler

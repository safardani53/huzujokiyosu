One of the most popular shows on HGTV, Good Bones follows mother-daughter duo Karen E. Laine and Mina Starsiak Hawk as they renovate and flip houses in Indiana. Karen and Mina also run a home rehab business called Two Chicks and a Hammer, Inc, and opened a home furnishing store, Two Chicks DIstrict Co., in 2020. Karen has since retired from the business to spend more time with her husband, Roger, and Mina is the proud mom of two children, Jack and Charlotte, with husband Steve Hawk.

On Sept. 6, 2022, a spinoff titled Good Bones: Risky Business premiered on HGTV. This six-episode series follows Mina as she remodels a rundown Indianapolis mansion.

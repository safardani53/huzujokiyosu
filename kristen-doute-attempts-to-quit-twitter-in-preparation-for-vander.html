Kristen Doute Attempts to Quit Twitter in Preparation For Vanderpump Rules Finale

February 14, 2024
blog

If you watched Vanderpump Rules Season 3 Episode 17, then you know that Kristen Doute may have finally hit rock bottom.

She’s been fired from SUR, she punched her own boyfriend at Scheana’s wedding, and all of her friends have abandoned her because she refuses to give up her mission to ruin Sandoval and Ariana’s relationship.

We knew things were bad when Kristen teamed up with Stassi (BTW, if you have any idea of why the hell Stassi gave her the time of day, please share in the comments.), but there’s an even more serious sign that the psychotic former SUR-ver may have lost it for good:

Kristen is quitting social media!!!!

Or, at least she says she’s going to. Like, right after this tweet, you guys.

Seriously in the last week, Kristen deleted her Instagram page and made the following announcement on Twitter:

"Taking a Twitter break for a bit, babes. Wishing everyone love and laughs on your dark days. Sending good vibes your way."

That "break" lasted about 18 hours, then she was right back to tweeting photos of selfies with James Kennedy and comparing herself and her boyfriend to Jay Z and Beyonce. (Seriously.)

As for Instagram, the break didn’t last much longer, but Kristen says she made a new set of rules for herself with regard to the photo-sharing site:

"I can master Instagram now…food, flowers, & the occasional selfie (every 6 months) so everyone can see how my hair is grown."

Yes, you can breathe deep, America. Our national nightmare is over, and Kristen’s hiatus from social media only lasted about as long as her split from James.

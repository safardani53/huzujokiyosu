What is World Darts Championship prize money 2024? The Sun

April 03, 2024
blog

THE World Darts Championship final takes place tonight and Luke Littler will be going for glory.

A whole host of stars have been in show over the last two weeks, including former world champions Michael van Gerwen, Raymond van Barneveld and Michael Smith. But 16-year-old Littler has usurped them all to reach this evening's final.

The tournament featured afternoon and evening sessions as players compete in seven rounds over a knockout format.

The total prize pool is £3.2 million, with players earning more the further they progress in the tournament.

When is the World Darts Championship final?

THIS year's final got underway tonight from 8.20pm at the Alexandra Palace.

What TV channel and live stream can I watch PDC World Darts Championship on?

YOU can catch all the action live on Sky Sports Darts in the UK.

Existing customers can stream through the Sky Go app, which is free to download on mobile devices.

Non-Sky customers can watch the tournament through Now TV, which does not require a contract.

In the US, the PDC World Darts Championship is available to watch on DAZN.

You can also live stream the action on the DAZN app.

A subscription to DAZN will set you back $19.99-a-month.
